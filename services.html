We use 7 steps cleaning process 

  • Mechanized Dewatering of WST
  • Removal of Sludge lying on the bottom of WST through special Sludge Pumps.
  • Cleaning of Internal Walls of WST through High Pressure Rotary Jets from Germany.
  • Vacuum Cleaning of the floor of WST through Industrial Vacuum Cleaners.
  • Spray of H2O2 inside the tanks as an Anti Bacterial Agent.
  • U V Radiation of the Tank.
  • Spray of bleaching powder in prescribed quantity after final cleaning of the tank.
